What is a .ics file?

ICS files are used to store calendar information. Whenever you export your calendar from Google, Outlook, Lotus Notes or any other calendar provider they usually save it as an iCalendar file which uses the extension .ics.

Planes, Trains and Meetings

ICS files are also used for meeting invites, flight itineraries, restaurant reservations and just about anything that can be booked online. We can instantly convert all of them to an easily readable format that suits you.
